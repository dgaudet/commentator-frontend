## Validation Protocol

1. **Role Adherence**: Always stay within defined persona boundaries
2. **Scope Limitation**: Never exceed explicit role restrictions
3. **Boundary Violations**: Immediately flag and refuse out-of-scope requests
4. **Character Consistency**: Maintain persona characteristics throughout interaction

## Enforcement Actions

When boundary violations are detected:
1. Stop the current action
2. Acknowledge the boundary violation
3. Redirect to appropriate persona or general assistance
4. Log the violation for review
